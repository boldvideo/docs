The Bold Video dashboard is your central hub for managing all your video content.

## Main Navigation

The left sidebar contains the main sections:

- **Dashboard** - Overview of your account activity and recent uploads
- **Videos** - All your uploaded videos
- **Collections** - Organized groups of videos
- **Settings** - Account and API configuration

## Dashboard Home

When you log in, the dashboard shows:

- **Recent uploads** - Your latest videos
- **Quick stats** - Total videos, views, and storage used
- **Activity feed** - Recent actions on your account

## Videos Section

### Browsing Videos

The Videos page shows all your uploaded content with:

- Thumbnail preview
- Title and duration
- Upload date
- View count
- Processing status (processing, ready, error)

### Filtering and Search

Use the toolbar to:

- **Search** by title or description
- **Filter** by status, collection, or date
- **Sort** by date, views, or title

### Video Actions

Click on any video to:

- **Play** - Preview the video
- **Edit** - Update title, description, and settings
- **Share** - Get embed codes and direct links
- **Delete** - Remove the video

## Settings

Access account settings for:

- **Profile** - Your name and email
- **API Keys** - Generate and manage API access tokens
- **Webhooks** - Configure event notifications
- **Billing** - Subscription and usage information
